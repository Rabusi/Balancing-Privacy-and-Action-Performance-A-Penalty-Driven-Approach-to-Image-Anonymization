Balancing Privacy and Action Performance

A Penalty-Driven Approach to Image Anonymization

Nazia Aslam1,2, Kamal Nasrollahi1,2,3
1Aalborg University, 2Pioneer Centre for AI, 3Milestone Systems
Paper Code

Abstract

We introduce a penalty-driven strategy for image anonymization that maintains high action recognition performance. Our method, compliant with GDPR and the EU AI Act, uses a novel feature-based penalty to protect private attributes while preserving task-critical information. Experiments show that performance is retained even with increased anonymization.
